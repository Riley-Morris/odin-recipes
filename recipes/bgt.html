French Baguette

A Classic French Baguette. Crisp on the outside, soft and chewy on the inside. Best served warm. Can be frozen

Ingredients

  • 1.75 ounces warm water (113 F)
  • 1.5 tsp yeast
  • 15oz bread flour
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 10 oz cool water

Directions:

  1. Measure the warm water in a small bowl and sprinkle the yeast on top. Set aside and allow the yeast to dissolve and become foamy.
  2. Measure the bread flour into a large bowl and stir in the salt.
  3. After letting yeast rise, Add the cool water, a little at a time, while stirring, just until a stiff, shaggy dough has formed (you may not need to use all of the water).
  4. Rest for 30 mins
  5. Shape into rectangle, fold into thirds, rotate and repeate twice
  6. Rest 2 hours
  7. Shape into baguettes, cover 45 mins
  8. Bake at 450 with a pan of boiling water until hollow sounding, about 35 mins
